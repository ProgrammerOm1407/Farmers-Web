TIPS FOR PRODUCT PHOTOGRAPHY:
============================
1. Use natural lighting when possible
2. Photograph products on a clean white or neutral background
3. Show the product clearly, focusing on texture and quality
4. Consider including some props like wooden spoons or fabric for context
5. Maintain consistent style across all product images
6. Ensure images are properly cropped and centered
